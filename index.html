Common Questions

Is this really free?
Yes. All services are completely free for IEHP members. There are no hidden costs, co-pays, or fees.
Do I have to be ready to quit today?
No. We meet you where you are. Whether you are ready to stop tomorrow or just thinking about it, we can help you figure out your next steps.
Can I quit vaping too?
Yes. Kick It California helps with both smoking and vaping.
Is my information private?
Yes. Your information is kept strictly confidential and is used only to help you with your quit journey.
What products do you offer?
We may provide free nicotine patches if they are medically appropriate for you. Your coach will discuss this during your call to see if you qualify.
